Documents & panels

Every Frettly document is built from one or more panels. Each panel shows a specific scale, chord or mode for the document’s chosen instrument and tuning.

You can think of a document as a notebook for a particular idea or project. Within that notebook, panels let you lay out different shapes, positions and concepts on the fretboard so they can be seen together.

  • Create new panels for scales, chords or modes.
  • Scroll up and down through panels in a single document.
  • Reorder panels by dragging them into a new position.
  • Save different documents for different learnings or projects.
