Left-handed & visual options

Frettly lets you flip an entire document between right- and left-handed layouts, and control how much information is shown on each panel.

  • Flip all panels at once from right-handed to left-handed (and back again).
  • Toggle between showing note names and interval labels, depending on what you want to focus on.
  • Hide certain notes or intervals to simplify the picture.
  • Click notes or intervals to hear them, either singly or with sustain.
