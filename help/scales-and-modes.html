Scales & modes

Scales and modes are at the heart of Frettly. Panels let you see how a scale lays across the entire neck, and how different modes relate to one another.

  • Select a scale or mode to see all its notes on the fretboard.
  • Create multiple panels in a document to compare related scales and modes.
  • Switch between note names and interval labels to see relationships more clearly.
  • Focus practice by zooming into one position, then zooming back out to see the bigger picture.
