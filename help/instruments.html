Instruments

Frettly can display scales/chord/modes and the notes with them, across the fretboard of several different instrument types, each of which comes with multiple available tunings. When the notes/intervals are clicked on the fretboard, Frettly plays the note pitch perfect and with samples which are instrument specific.

  • Guitar (six string)
  • Guitar (seven string)
  • Bass Guitar (four string)
  • Bass Guitar (five string)
  • Bass Guitar (six string)
  • Banjo (five string)
  • Banjo (four string)
  • Mandolin (four string)
  • Ukulele (four string)
