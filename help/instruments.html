Instruments and tunings

Frettly can display scales, chords, and modes — and the notes within them — across the fretboards of different instrument types, each of which includes multiple tunings. When you click a note or interval on the fretboard, Frettly plays the note pitch-perfect, in the octave and string position you clicked, using instrument-specific samples.
