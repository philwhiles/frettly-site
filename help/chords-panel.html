Chords panel

The chords panel lets you lay out multiple chord shapes and voicings so you can compare how they sit on the fretboard.

  • Select a chord type and see it drawn on the fretboard.
  • Add additional chords or voicings to the same document for side-by-side comparison.
  • Scroll horizontally if a chord shape extends further up the neck.
  • Save a document of favourite shapes for a particular tune or key.
