Instruments & tunings

Frettly supports Guitar, Bass Guitar, Mandolin, Banjo and Ukulele. Each document is tied to a chosen instrument and tuning, and all panels update together when you change that choice.

  • Set the instrument for a document when you first create it.
  • Choose from standard tunings or alternate tunings for that instrument.
  • When you change tuning, all panels in the document refresh to reflect the new layout.
  • Keep separate documents for different instruments, tunings or projects.
