Fretboard navigation

Frettly’s fretboard panels are designed to be explored. You can move around the neck, zoom in for detail, or zoom out to see the full pattern at once.

  • Scroll horizontally to move along the fretboard.
  • Use zoom controls to focus on a smaller region or see the full neck.
  • Combine scrolling and zooming to isolate tricky areas for practice.
  • When a document has many panels, scroll vertically to move between them.
