Why Go Pro?

Frettler began as a personal project for exploring the guitar through clear visualisation, practical music theory and interactive sound.

With Frettler Pro, you can combine multiple scale, mode and chord fretboards within reusable documents, create chord organisers, explore different tunings and build a personal library that supports your playing, learning and songwriting.

Your purchase unlocks the complete current feature set while also supporting the continued development of Frettler.

When you go Pro, you’re getting more than just the current feature set — you’re also supporting ongoing development and helping to shape where Frettler goes next.

What You Get with Frettler Pro

  • Create fretboards using all 14 scale types, 9 modes and 44 chord types currently included with Frettler.
  • Use the complete collection of instrument tunings.
  • Create and save documents containing multiple fretboards and chord organisers.
  • Explore patterns interactively with note marking, interval display and audio playback.
  • Receive bug fixes, performance improvements and refinements to existing features at no additional cost.
