Why Go Pro?

Frettly started life as a personal project to help me understand the guitar through clear visualisation, interactivity and application of music theory. Frettly Pro is the document-based realisation of that desire, allowing you to save multiple scale/chord/mode fretboards within individual documents — built for players who want to explore scales, chords and tunings with flexible features and interactive audio.

When you go Pro, you’re getting more than just the current feature set — you’re also supporting ongoing development and helping to shape where Frettly goes next.

What You Get with Frettly Pro

  • Access to the full Pro feature set available today.
  • All current instrument tunings, plus future tuning additions.
  • Create fretboards with the full catalogue of 14 Scale types, 9 Modes, and 44 Chord types — with more added over time.
  • Bug fixes, performance improvements, and refinements to existing features.
