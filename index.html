Frettly - the comprehensive fretboard explorer

Frettly is a macOS application dedicated to enabling players of fretted instruments to visualise, experiment and explore scales, modes and chords in a document-like canvas.

Supporting Guitar, Bass Guitar, Mandolin, Banjo and Ukulele, each document you create can show multiple scales/chords/modes for the document’s chosen instrument, and each instrument type supports multiple possible tunings.

Built for: players who like to see the relationships between notes, intervals, and shapes laid out clearly.

A document-based fretboard notebook

  • Frettly documents are made of panels, each displaying a given scale, chord or mode
  • Scroll up/down in the document or “Jump to” a specific panel in a document
  • Frettly panels can be dragged within the document to re-arrange them
  • Fretboards can be horizontally scrolled
  • Fretboards can be zoomed in/out
  • Flip all the panels in a document from right to left handed
  • Modify a document’s instrument/tuning and all panels update to show that instrument
  • Notes or intervals displayed can be selectively hidden
  • Easily flip between displaying notes or intervals
  • Toggle the display of either the notes or the intervals
  • Click any note or interval to hear the notes singly or with sustain
  • Save different documents for different learnings, projects or tunings.
If you’re a fellow guitar theory tinkerer, your feedback on workflows and visualisation will be especially welcome.
