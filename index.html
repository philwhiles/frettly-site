Frettly - the interactive fretboard explorer and notebook

Frettly is a macOS application dedicated to enabling players of fretted instruments to visualise, experiment, and explore scales, modes and chords using a document based canvas.

Think of Frettly as Pages, not for text but for fretboards.

Frettly isn't just a scale and chord library - it's a persistent, document-based workspace designed by a player, for players. Whether you are composing a complex jazz piece, teaching a student, or exploring a new tuning on your banjo, Frettly organises your theory explorations into easily read, playable, shareable and saveable documents.


Supporting guitar, bass guitar, mandolin, banjo and ukulele , each document you create can show multiple scales/chords/modes for the document’s chosen instrument, and each instrument type supports multiple possible tunings.

The roadmap for future Frettly enhancements is brimming with features. If you have suggestions for features or improvements, need support or advice, contact us at:

hello@frettly.app

Built for: teachers and players alike, who want to explore the relationships between notes/intervals in scales and chords, train their ears and to mark and annotate their learnings in reloadable documents.

What you can do with Frettly

  • Build visual music documents from fretboards, scales, modes, chords and chord organisers, all arranged on a flexible document canvas
  • Switch instruments, tunings or handedness at any time and watch every fretboard and chord panel adapt to the new context
  • Keep separate documents for lessons, songs, theory explorations, practice routines, alternate tunings or ideas you want to return to later
  • Listen to notes, scale patterns, modes and strummed chords with pitch-perfect, instrument-specific playback as you explore the fretboard
  • Focus your learning by showing notes, intervals, marked notes, or only the parts of a pattern you want to study right now
  • Highlight important notes and intervals, add your own panel notes, and capture teaching points, discoveries, reminders and practice ideas
  • Rearrange, duplicate, copy and move musical ideas freely, both within a single Frettly document and between different documents
  • Personalise the look of your workspace with fretboard backgrounds and note colour themes, making documents clearer and more enjoyable to use
