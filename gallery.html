Gallery

A few example scenarios showing how Frettly lays out scales, chords and tunings across the fretboard. Most screenshots are taken on a 16" MacBook Pro, so they show full-width panels as you’d see them in the app.

Exploring a scale across the full fretboard

A single panel showing a scale across the full neck, with notes highlighted so you can see patterns and shapes at a glance.

Comparing chord voicings in stacked panels

Multiple panels in a single document, each showing different chord shapes or voicings for quick comparison and practice.

Alternate tunings and left-handed layouts

Panels updated for alternate tunings, flipped between right- and left-handed views so you can work the way you play.
